About

What drives me

Technology earns its seat when it changes the number on the board's page. I have spent fifteen years in the operating core of organizations where the platform is the business, and I have learned that the hardest part is never the software. It is getting a room of capable executives to commit to one decision, then staying accountable until the result shows up.

I take on the hard, multi-vendor, high-stakes programs others stall on, bring AI in where it removes real cost and risk, and stay accountable through go-live and benefits realization. AI has made executive governance more consequential, not less, and that is the work I want more of.

I am at my best when the stakes are high, the room is divided, and the outcome has to be real.
